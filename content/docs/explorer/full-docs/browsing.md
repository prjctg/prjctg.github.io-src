# Browsing Content

Anı⃰mr gives you several ways to find songs and animations. You can start from the home page, browse dedicated libraries, or search for something specific.

---

## The Discover page

The home page (**Discover**) is organised into zones that each show different kinds of content.

### Personal zone

If you have played anything before, this zone appears at the top. It shows:

- A **resume card** for your most recent session, so you can pick up exactly where you left off.
- **Jump Back In** — a row of songs you have played recently.
- **Your Recent Anims** — animations you have watched recently.

This zone only appears once you have some history on the device. It is not shown to first-time visitors.

### Latest Songs and Latest Anims

These rows show the most recently added songs and animations on the platform. They update automatically as new content is published. Click **See all** to go to the full browser for that content type.

### You Might Like

A mixed row of randomly selected songs and animations. Use the **Shuffle** button to get a fresh set of picks at any time.

### Community zone

At the bottom of the page, you will find:

- Songs and animations that still need contributions — a good way to see what the community is working on.
- A leaderboard of top contributors.

---

## The Songs browser

Go to **Songs** in the navigation to browse the full song library. Three tabs give you different views:

| Tab | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **Latest** | Songs added most recently, with the newest at the top |
| **Random** | A random selection of songs from the library |
| **Search** | A search box to find songs by title or artist name |

Click any song in the list to open its detail page, then choose an animation to start watching.

---

## The Anims browser

Go to **Anims** in the navigation to browse all animations across all songs. Three tabs give you different views:

| Tab | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **Latest** | Animations added most recently |
| **Random** | A random selection of animations |
| **Search** | A search box to find animations by name |

Each animation card shows the animation name, the creator's username, and a tag indicating the type of animation (for example: Karaoke, Fretboard, Keyboard, Game, Sheet Music, Visualiser). Cards with thumbnail images show a preview; others show a type icon.

Click an animation card to go directly to the player with that animation loaded.

---

## How search works

In both the Songs and Anims browsers, select the **Search** tab and type in the search box. You can search by:

- Song title or artist name (in the Songs browser)
- Animation name (in the Anims browser)

Press **Enter** or click the **Search** button to run the search. Results appear below the search box.

## Quick orientation

The main navigation has three sections relevant to Explorers:

- **Discover** — the home page, with curated rows of content
- **Songs** — browse or search the full song library
- **Anims** — browse or search the full animation library

Once you pick a song and an animation, the player opens automatically. Controls appear at the bottom of the screen.

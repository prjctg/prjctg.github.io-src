# Explorer Full Documentation

Welcome to the complete guide for exploring Anı⃰mr. This page covers every control and setting available in the player.

---

## The Player Page

The player page (`/g/...`) is the main experience. It shows the animation in the foreground, with the song playing in sync. Controls appear as overlays when you hover over the screen or tap on mobile.

---

## Playback Controls

| Control | Description |
|---|---|
| **Play / Pause** | Start or pause playback. You can also press **Space** on the keyboard. |
| **Progress bar** | Shows elapsed and total time. Click or drag to seek to any position. |
| **Playback rate** | Speeds up or slows down both audio and animation. Options typically include 0.5×, 0.75×, 1×, 1.25×, and 1.5×. |
| **Volume / Mute** | Adjust audio volume or toggle mute. |
| **Full screen** | Expand the animation to fill your screen. Press **Escape** or the button again to exit. |

---

## Switching Songs and Animations

### Song Selector

The song selector lets you pick which song to play. Animations are synchronized to the selected song, so switching songs will also update the available animations.

### Animation Selector

The animation selector lists all animations available for the current song. Each animation is a different visual experience set to the same music. Switch animations mid-song — the new animation will load and sync to the current playback position without interrupting audio.

---

## Animation Settings

Some animations expose configurable parameters. Click the **Settings** (gear) icon to open the settings panel, then select the **Animation** tab.

Parameters vary by animation and may include things like:

- **Lyrics channel** — which lyric stream or language to display
- **Color or style options** — visual theme choices
- **Numeric values** — intensity, speed, or size controls
- **Toggles** — show or hide specific visual elements

Changes take effect immediately. A **Reset to Defaults** button restores the animation's original settings.

---

## Display Settings

The settings panel also contains display options that apply globally (not per-animation).

### Video Background

Anı⃰mr can display the song's music video behind the animation. In the **General** tab of the settings panel:

- **Load video** — toggle whether the video background is loaded at all
- **Video variant** — if a song has multiple video sources, choose which one to use
- **Background fit** — how the video is sized: `fill` (covers the full area), `contain` (letterboxed), or `none`
- **Background opacity** — blend the video from fully transparent to fully visible

### Dark / Light Mode

A sun/moon icon in the top navigation toggles between dark and light interface themes. Your preference is saved automatically.

---

## Browsing Songs and Animations

### Song Browser (`/s`)

Browse all available songs. Use the search bar to find by title or artist. Click a song to open its detail page, then choose an animation to watch.

### Animation Browser (`/a`)

Browse all animations across all songs. Each card shows the animation name and the song it belongs to.

### Featured Content

The home page (`/`) shows a curated selection of featured songs and animations. This is the fastest way to discover what the platform has to offer.

## Keyboard Shortcuts

| Key | Action |
|---|---|
| `Space` | Play / Pause |
| `F` | Toggle full screen |
| `←` / `→` | Seek backward / forward |

---

## Tips

- **Seamless animation switching** — you can change animations without pausing the music. The new animation loads in the background and swaps in smoothly.
- **Resume on return** — if you leave and come back, the player remembers your last song and animation.
- **Mobile** — tap the screen to show or hide controls.

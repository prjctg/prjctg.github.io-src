# About Anı⃰mr

Anı⃰mr is a platform where music and animation come together. You can sit back and watch animations play in perfect sync with songs, or get involved by contributing song data and building your own animations. Whether you just want to enjoy the experience or help create it, there is a place for you here.

---

## Three ways to use Anı⃰mr

**Explorer** — Browse songs and animations, hit play, and enjoy the show. Discover new content on the home page, search for songs you love, and adjust the experience to suit your taste.

**Music Editor** — Help grow the library by contributing song data. This includes adding lyrics, timing information, and other details that power the animations.

**Anim Designer / Developer** — Build custom animations that respond to the music. Animations are coded against a rich set of music events — beats, lyrics, chords, and more — so the visuals move with the song.

---

## A community project

Anı⃰mr is a work in progress, shaped by everyone who contributes to it. Every song submitted and every animation added makes the platform richer for everyone. If you are curious about getting started, the guides in this documentation will walk you through each path.

# Anim Designer Guide

Welcome! The Anim Designer is a visual tool for building animations on Animr. You pick ready-made modules, arrange how they appear on screen, and set any options you want — all without writing a single line of code.

---

## What is the Anim Designer?

An animation on Animr is made up of one or more **modules** — self-contained visual effects that respond to the music. The Anim Designer lets you assemble those modules into a finished animation by working through three steps:

1. **Pick your modules** — Choose from published code modules or existing animations to include in your animation.
2. **Arrange the layout** — Decide how multiple modules are positioned on screen: stacked, side by side, rows, picture-in-picture, or other presets.
3. **Configure parameters** — Expose adjustable controls (such as color choices, display style, or lyric track) so viewers can personalize the experience when they watch.

---

## Who is it for?

Anyone who wants to create a visual experience on Animr without coding. If you enjoy combining existing building blocks and experimenting with how they look together, the Anim Designer is your starting point.

---

## How to open the Anim Designer

You can reach the designer in two ways:

- **From the Playground** — Go to the Playground, select the **Anim** mode, and click the **Design** tab. The Playground is a safe space to experiment: nothing is published until you choose to save it to the server.
- **From an animation entry** — Open any animation you own and click the **Design** tab in the editor.
